Step 1: Choose Your Weapon

The first step is seemingly simple but incredibly important: choose the right bubble gum. You may have to do some experiminting here to see what gum best fits your style. No amount of time spent testing gums could be considered too much, so study the choices thoroughly.

Although any gum will work for blowing bubbles, some are better than others. Choosing a brand specifically intended for blowing bubbles will help to ensure your success.

For this guide, we choose a Bubbilicious Strawberry splash flavor. It has remarkable bubble-blowability and juicy-good flavor.
