Step 5: Buh-buh-buh-BLOW

Now, form the gum around your tongue to make the basic bubble shape.

Once you have the shape correct inside of your mouth, force just the tip of it out of your lips and blow into the bubble.

The bubble may pop on your face leaving you with sitcky bubble gum all over your lips.
This is normal.
